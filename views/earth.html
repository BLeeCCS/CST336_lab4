Welcome to Earth!

Earth is the third planet from the sun. The only planet known to contain free oxygen, oceans of water on its surface to support life. Earth is not perfectly round. It has roughly 8000 miles in diameter. Water covers roughly 71% of the surface. According to radiometric dating estimation and other evidence, Earth formed over 4.5 billion years ago.
